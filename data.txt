ibex positions itself as an elite Customer Experience (CX) outsourcer for the world's leading brands, including startups, scale-ups, and blue-chip companies. Their core value proposition revolves around "BPO 2.0," which they describe as the future of CX, available now. The company aims to build powerful customer engagement and insight solutions designed to protect client investment, mitigate financial and operational risk, and accelerate ROI. They describe themselves as a "perfectly disruptive synthesis of people, technology, and expertise," delivering highly customized, flexible, and scalable CX programs for dynamic and discerning brands. Their guiding philosophy is being a "people-helping-people company." They expertly manage over 138 million interactions each year for their clients.

Section ID: 1.1
Title: BPO 2.0 Explained
Tags: BPO 2.0, future of CX, innovation, ROI

BPO 2.0 is ibex's forward-looking framework for Customer Experience outsourcing. It represents a move beyond traditional business process outsourcing to a model that is:

Customer Inspired & Client Driven: Solutions are built with the end-customer in mind but are strategically driven by the client's business goals.

Focused on ROI: It aims to accelerate return on investment by building solutions that protect client investment and mitigate financial and operational risk.

Integrated & Insight-Led: It leverages data and analytics to create a connected lifecycle that empowers the end-to-end customer experience.

ibex structures its offerings into three interconnected pillars, creating a connected lifecycle that empowers the end-to-end customer experience: ibex Connect, ibex Digital, and ibex CX.

Section ID: 2.1
Title: ibex Connect: Omnichannel Service & Support
Tags: ibex Connect, customer care, omnichannel, support, contact center

This pillar focuses on delivering elite outsourced customer care. Key features include:

Customer Sales & Support: Managing customer interactions across sales and support channels.

Global Talent Network: Access to a diverse, global pool of highly trained agents.

Award-Winning Agents: Emphasis on the quality and performance of their customer service representatives.

Customized Contact Centers: Flexible engagement models tailored to specific client needs.

Performance Technology: Utilizing technology to enhance agent performance and customer experience.

Deep Information Security: Ensuring robust data protection and compliance (PCI/HIPAA certified).

Section ID: 2.2
Title: ibex Digital: Digital Customer Acquisition
Tags: ibex Digital, digital marketing, customer acquisition, advertising

This pillar is designed to drive online customer acquisition through digital channels. Capabilities include:

Search & Social Advertising: Managing paid advertising campaigns on platforms like Google and social media networks.

Digital Order Orchestration: Streamlining and managing the digital order process from click to conversion.

Precision Conversion Attribution: Using advanced analytics to accurately attribute conversions to the correct marketing touchpoints.

Payment Distribution: Handling the complexities of payment processing in the acquisition funnel.

Bid Optimization: Using algorithms and expertise to optimize bids for advertising spend efficiency.

Section ID: 2.3
Title: ibex CX: Surveys & Feedback Analytics
Tags: ibex CX, feedback, surveys, analytics, data visualization, VOC

This pillar is focused on understanding and improving the customer experience through data. Core offerings include:

All-Channel Digital Customer Outreach: Engaging customers for feedback across various digital channels.

Lifecycle Touchpoint Outreach: Surveying customers at key points throughout their journey.

Email, Text, & Web CX Surveys: Deploying surveys via multiple channels to capture feedback.

5-Star Surveys: Gathering standardized ratings to measure satisfaction.

Real-Time Issue Resolution: Identifying and addressing customer issues as they are reported in feedback.

Data Analytics & Visualization: Transforming raw feedback data into actionable insights through analysis and visual reports.

Social Media Monitoring: Tracking and analyzing brand sentiment and customer conversations on social media.
